Product Designer / Code Developer

I design and develop applications that enable people to communicate, transact and learn. As a John S. Knight Fellow at Stanford (2014), I researched and prototyped systems to discover and personalize news content. I have built products for news, cyber security, crowdsourcing, search and mobile payment applications.

My design practice is based on understanding and responding to a user’s experience. This is an iterative dialogue that translates the users’ needs into a more engaging and effective usage experience. As a developer, my approach is 100% lean startup, based on rapid development cycles and continuous feedback.
